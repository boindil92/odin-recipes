Now comes the question: what is Is Indian Chicken Curry?

Chicken curry from the Indian subcontinent typically features chicken,

stewed in a tomato-based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices.

This recipe, like many others, calls for curry powder

The "bricks" of our dish

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

The "to do list"

(to the well organized minds: Indian-Chicken-Curry.exe)

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion until lightly browned.

  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt.

    Continue stirring for 2 minutes.

  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk.

    Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.

  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper.

    Simmer 5 more minutes.

"Indian Chicken Curry" is ready, Serve hot and enjoy!
